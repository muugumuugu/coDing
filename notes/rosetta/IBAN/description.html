{{wikipedia}} The   wp:InternationalBankAccountNumber|International Bank Account Number (IBAN)]">[wp:Transcriptionerror|transcription errors">wp:InternationalBankAccountNumber|International Bank Account Number (IBAN)]   is an internationally agreed means of identifying bank accounts across national borders with a reduced risk of propagating [[wp:Transcriptionerror|transcription errors. The IBAN consists of up to 34 alphanumeric characters: ::*   first the two-letter ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code
::*   then two check digits, and ::*   finally a country-specific Basic Bank Account Number (BBAN). The check digits enable a sanity check of the bank account number to confirm its integrity even before submitting a transaction.
