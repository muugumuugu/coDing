Every symmetric, positive definite matrix A can be decomposed into a product of a unique lower triangular matrix L and its transpose: :A = LL^T

L

is called the Cholesky factor of A, and can be interpreted as a generalized square root of A, as described in wp:Cholesky decomposition|Cholesky decomposition.
