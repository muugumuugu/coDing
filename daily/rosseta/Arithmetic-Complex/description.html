A   wp:Complex number|complex number   is a number which can be written as: a + b \times i (sometimes shown as: b + a \times i where   a   and   b  are real numbers,   and   wp:Imaginary_unit|i   is   √{{overline| -1 }} Typically, complex numbers are represented as a pair of real numbers called the "imaginary part" and "real part",   where the imaginary part is the number to be multiplied by i.
