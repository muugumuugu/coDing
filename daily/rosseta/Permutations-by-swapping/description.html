Task: Generate permutations of n items in which successive permutations differ from each other by the swapping of any two items. Also generate the sign of the permutation which is +1 when the permutation is generated from an even number of swaps from the initial state, and -1 for odd. Show the permutations and signs of three items, in order of generation here. Such data are of use in generating the Matrix arithmetic|determinant of a square matrix and any functions created should bear this in mind. Note: The Steinhaus–Johnson–Trotter algorithm generates successive permutations where adjacent items are swapped, but from wp:Parityofa_permutation#Example|this discussion adjacency is not a requirement.
