There are four types of common coins in   https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States US   currency: :::#   quarters   (25 cents) :::#   dimes   (10 cents) :::#   nickels   (5 cents),   and :::#   pennies   (1 cent) There are six ways to make change for 15 cents: :::#   A dime and a nickel :::#   A dime and 5 pennies :::#   3 nickels :::#   2 nickels and 5 pennies :::#   A nickel and 10 pennies :::#   15 pennies

Task: How many ways are there to make change for a dollar using these common coins?     (1 dollar = 100 cents).
Optional: Less common are dollar coins (100 cents);   and very rare are half dollars (50 cents).   With the addition of these two coins, how many ways are there to make change for $1000? (Note:   the answer is larger than   232).
